**ABC Training Center Analytics Dashboard**

**Project Overview**

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze student enrollment, revenue generation, and course performance for a training institute. The dashboard helps identify enrollment trends, top-performing courses, and business growth opportunities through data visualization and KPI tracking.

**Tools & Technologies Used**
Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel.

**Dashboard Features**

KPI cards for Total Revenue, Student Count, and Course Performance. Monthly enrollment and revenue trend analysis. Age-group and city-wise student segmentation. Interactive slicers for Year, City, and Course filtering. Dynamic visualizations for business insights and reporting

**Key Insights**

Identified top-performing courses based on revenue and enrollments. Analyzed seasonal enrollment trends across different periods. Compared student distribution by city and age groups. Improved reporting visibility using interactive dashboards
